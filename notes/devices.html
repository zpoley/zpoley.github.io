Google Pixel 3a

It's been years since I've been willing to spend big money on a phone. My last phone was an Axon ZTE-7 which I was pretty happy with at the time because it felt like a pretty good deal for $399, however after spending a couple of years with the ZTE-7 I grew tired of the custom Android build and lack of updates that were happening in Google's mainstream Android.

It has been amazing to be on mainstream Android with the Pixel 3a. The Google Pixel phones have finally become what I consider to be more competitive with Apple phones, and the price of the 3a for the specs and mainstream Android distribution are well worth $399. Especially having come from the Axon.
